Welcome to my website

Hello and welcome to my site! I’m Soroban, a developer you may have come across somewhere on the internet. This website is a place where I share my work, projects, and experiments.

I spend a lot of my time building games and other software, usually working on ideas that interest me or help me learn something new. Some projects are small tools, while others are longer-term or more experimental.

Outside of development, I’m also a drum & bass artist. Music is something I work on alongside programming, and both end up influencing how I spend my time creating and learning.

Feel free to explore the site to see what I’ve been working on.
